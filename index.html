Train Like a Martial Artist

Kenpo is a technique-based martial art that teaches self-defense through real world-scenarios. Its scalable system helps students respond appropriately to escalatin situations, using effective techniques that incorporate all the bodies natural weapons(hands, feet, elbows, knees, etc.). Students will probably not face these kinds of scenarios, but if they do, they will be glad to know how to handle it.

About American Kenpo Karate

The type of karate we teach is a variation of Ed Parker's American Kenpo. Ed Parker developed this martial art designed for real-world defense. He continually refined techniques based on modern fighding situations and emphasized scientific principles over mystical one like ki. Ed Parker wanted to create an art that tailored to anyone (men, women, and children).
