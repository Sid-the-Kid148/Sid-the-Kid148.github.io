Our Classes

Arnott Kenpo Karate offers different kinds of programs differing from beginners to experienced. There is a class for you if you want to compete, increse fitness, or just to learn Kenpo.

Mighty Tigers

A fun introduction to martial arts for younger students. These classes are where students can learn basic Kenpo skills and learn other skills in the process like coordination, respect, and teamwork.

Kids Beginners

Students learn the basic fundamentals of Kenpo like stances and different strikes. They also start their different techniques on their card.

Kids Color Belt

These are where the students who are more advanced build on their skills with others. First an instructor leads them through warm-ups and then they break off into groups of people with the same belt rank to work on their curriculum. Then we end with controlled, supervised sparring.

Adult Group

This class is where adult students of all ranks work to build their Kenpo skills. Like the Kids class, there is warm-up, group work on curriculum, then ending with sparring.

Point Fighting

Available to people of all ranks. You develop speed, timing, different combinations that can help you in any form of sparring. You do drills that help you with these skills and gets you ready for upcoming tournaments.

Eskrima

This class is where you learn the Filipino martial art of using Eskrima sticks. There are two different classes: fundamentals and sparring. You learn the basics of using these weapons and how effective they can be. Then with controlled and supervised attention, people spar each other with the sticks with light contact with full armor
